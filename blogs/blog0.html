What is this all about

I've recently started a long project that requires me to focus my reading/thinking on a very narrow topic. This blog is an attempt and a vent to learn about other broader topics I'm interested in, primarily by writing about them. It's the kind of writing that aims to understand rather than to inform. Every 2-3 months I will explore a topic that I virtually know nothing about and then document this learning journey in longish piece. Topics will range from broad technical (e.g. energy storage, blockchain...), to intersections of economics and industry, to skills I'm trying to learn (e.g. analysing visual arts/fiction). Aside from this self-imposed pedagogy, I'll occasionally write personal reflections or random thoughts of (hopefully) some value. To many intriguing stories, Omar Jan 8, 2022
