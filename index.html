About

I've recently started a long project that requires me to focus my all reading/thinking on a very narrow field. This blog is an attempt to learn about other broader topics I'm interested in, primarily by writing about them. It's the kind of writing that aims to understand rather than to inform.

Every 2-3 months I will explore a topic that I virtually know nothing about and then document this learning journey in a long piece/essay/blog. Aside from this self-imposed pedagogy, I'll occasionally write personal reflections or random thoughts of (hopefully) some value.


I'm also happy to hear feedback, discuss interesting projects, and recieve articles & book recommendations: email, twitter, or linkedin.
